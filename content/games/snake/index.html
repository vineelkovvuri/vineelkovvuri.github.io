🐍 Snake

Eat the apples to grow longer.
Don't hit the walls or your own tail!

Score

Length

How to play
• Steer the snake with ← ↑ → ↓, pause with P.
• On touch screens use the arrow buttons below the board.
• Eat the red apple to grow and score points.
• You cannot reverse straight back into yourself.
• The snake speeds up every few apples (level up).
• Crashing into a wall or your tail ends the game. Your best score is saved as your Record.
