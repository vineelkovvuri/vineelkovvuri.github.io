How to play
• Move your paddle with ↑ ↓ or the mouse, pause with P.
• On touch screens drag on the board or use the buttons below.
• The ball speeds up the longer a rally lasts.
• Score a point when the ball gets past your opponent.
• First player to 7 points wins the match.
• Your total Wins are saved between sessions.
