and smash all the bricks!

How to play
• Move the paddle with ← → or the mouse, pause with P.
• On touch screens drag on the board or use the buttons below.
• Keep the ball in play — it bounces off walls, the paddle and bricks.
• Break every brick to clear the level; harder bricks take more hits.
• You have 3 lives; lose one each time the ball falls past the paddle.
• Your best score is saved as your Record.
