Find the different color 1

One square has a slightly different shade.
Tap it as fast as you can!
10 rounds — each one gets trickier.

How it works
• Every square is the same color — except one, which is a slightly different shade.
• Tap the odd square to score a point and move to the next round.
• Each round the grid grows and the shade difference gets smaller, so it gets harder.
• Play 10 rounds, then see your medal, stars and score.
• Your best score is saved as your Record on this device.
