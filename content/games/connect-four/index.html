Red's turn

How to play
• Click or tap a column to drop your disc into the lowest empty slot.
• Players alternate: Red 🔴 goes first, then Yellow 🟡.
• Line up four discs in a row — horizontally, vertically or diagonally — to win.
• Play against a simple Computer or a friend in 2 Players mode.
• If the board fills with no winner, it's a draw. Your wins vs the computer are saved.
