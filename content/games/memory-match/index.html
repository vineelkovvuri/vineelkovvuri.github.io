🃏 Memory Match

How to play
• Click or tap a card to flip it and reveal the emoji.
• Flip two cards — if they match they stay face up, otherwise they flip back.
• Find all the matching pairs to win.
• Try to finish in the fewest moves and fastest time.
• Pick a difficulty for a bigger grid. Your best move count per difficulty is saved as your Record.
