🎵 Simon

Watch the sequence,
then repeat it!

How to play
• Press Start. Simon lights up a color — watch carefully.
• Repeat the sequence by tapping the colored pads in the same order.
• Each round adds one more color to remember.
• In Strict mode a single mistake ends the game; otherwise you get to retry the round.
• How many rounds can you memorize? Your best round is saved as your Record.
