🐤 Flappy Bird

Tap, click or press Space to flap.
Fly through the gaps in the pipes!

How to play
• Tap the board, click FLAP, or press Space / ↑ to flap upward.
• Pause with P.
• Each flap gives the bird a little lift; gravity always pulls it down.
• Steer through the gaps between the green pipes.
• Score 1 point for every pipe you pass.
• Touching a pipe, the ground or the ceiling ends the game. Your best score is saved as your Record.
