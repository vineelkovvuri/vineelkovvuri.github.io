How to play
• Move your ship with ← →, fire with Space, pause with P.
• On touch screens use the buttons below the board.
• Destroy every alien to clear the wave; each new wave is faster.
• Aliens drop bombs — dodge them or lose a life.
• You have 3 lives. The game ends if aliens reach you or you run out.
• Your best score is saved as your Record.
