How to play
• Rotate with ← →, thrust with ↑, fire with Space, pause with P.
• On touch screens use the buttons below the board.
• Your ship drifts with momentum and wraps around the screen edges.
• Shooting a big asteroid splits it into smaller, faster ones.
• Clear every asteroid to advance to the next wave.
• You have 3 lives; colliding with an asteroid costs one. Your best score is saved as your Record.
