🔍 Multi-Search Highlighter

Features
• Ctrl+Click any word to highlight all its occurrences in a unique color
• Select text first, then Ctrl+Click to highlight multi-word phrases
• Ctrl+Click a highlighted word again to remove its highlight
• Click a color chip in the status bar to remove that highlight
• 17 distinct highlight colors, auto-assigned
• Highlights update automatically as you edit the text
• Overview ruler shows highlight positions in the scrollbar
