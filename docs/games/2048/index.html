🔢 2048

How to play
• Slide tiles with ← ↑ → ↓ or the arrow buttons.
• On touch screens swipe on the board in any direction.
• When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one.
• Reach the 2048 tile to win — then keep going for a higher score!
• The game ends when the board is full and no moves remain.
• Your best score is saved as your Record.
