🔨 Whack-a-Mole

Bonk the moles that pop up.
Avoid the bombs 💣 — 30 seconds on the clock!

How to play
• Click or tap a mole 🐹 the moment it pops out of its hole.
• Each mole you bonk scores points; faster reactions keep the combo going.
• Watch out for bombs 💣 — hitting one costs you points!
• The moles get quicker as the clock ticks down.
• You have 30 seconds. Your best score is saved as your Record.
