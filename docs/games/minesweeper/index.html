💣 Minesweeper

How to play
• Click a tile to reveal it. Numbers show how many mines touch that tile.
• Right-click (or turn on Flag mode and tap) to place a 🚩 flag on a suspected mine.
• On touch screens, use the Flag button to switch between digging and flagging.
• The first tile you open is always safe.
• Reveal every safe tile to win — hit a mine and it's game over.
• Your fastest clear per difficulty is saved as your Best Time.
