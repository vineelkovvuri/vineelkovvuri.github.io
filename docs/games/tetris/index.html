How to play
• Move the falling piece with ← →, rotate with ↑.
• ↓ soft drop, Space hard drop, P pause.
• On touch screens use the buttons below the board.
• Fill an entire row to clear it and score points.
• Clearing 4 rows at once is a Tetris and scores the most.
• The game speeds up every 10 lines (level up). Your best score is saved as your Record.
